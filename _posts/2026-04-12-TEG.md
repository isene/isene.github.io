After more than 40 years of research into Free Will and Mathematics, I have released my work on [Trans-Existential Grounding (TEG)](https://isene.com/freewill).

The [academic paper](https://philpapers.org/rec/ISEAMF) was accepted on PhilPapers, marking a step toward a popular science book, "The Freedom Proof".

I've written several articles on Free Will over the years, including the [OnePageBook](https://isene.org/onepagebooks/#1pb-3-free-will). But last summer I got the real breakthrough. Since then I've been busy formulating the mathematics and ensuring it is sound, and in parallel writing the upcoming book.

![TEG route 1](/assets/posts/teg1.png)

The academic paper is mathematics-heavy, while the book takes a very different approach. It focuses on the reason for existence, your choices and personal responsibility.

Stay tuned for progress on this project.
